## Brand & Style
The design system for Adelaide Maria's 80th birthday is inspired by the serene and timeless landscape of the **Serra da Bocaina** and the rustic refinement of the **Valle Chico Mineiro**. The visual theme, **Eucalyptus & Champagne Gold**, combines natural botanical freshness with the classic luxury of gold leaf.

It is soft, organic, yet extremely premium. The presence of eucalyptus leaves, watercolor textures, thin gold geometric borders, and elements inspired by traditional letterpress stationery creates a warm, high-end editorial experience.

## Colors
- **Deep Eucalyptus (#1F3A31):** The primary color, representing the ancient forests and quiet strength. Used for high-contrast typography, headers, and major action states.
- **Sage Green (#7A9A82):** A soft, calming green used for details, separators, and secondary components.
- **Antique Gold (#D4AF37):** Used for highlighting special accents, borders, interactive states, and metadata tags, suggesting elegance and value.
- **Champagne Paper (#FBF9F4):** The base background color, resembling thick, textured high-end stationery.
- **Charcoal Spruce (#1C2522):** The main body text color, offering soft legibility.

## Typography
- **EB Garamond:** Used for headlines and titles. Reflects literary depth, heritage, and classic authority.
- **Playfair Display (Italic):** Used for manuscript quotes, subtitles, and expressive labels.
- **Source Serif 4:** Clean, readable serif for content body and forms.
- **Literata (Uppercase, Spaced):** Used for metadata, labels, and badges to add structure.
